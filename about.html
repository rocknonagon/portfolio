About me

I’m an illustrator living in Melbourne. This website is a work in progress so check back in a few weeks and it will be different!

I think the more you look at something, the more you see in it, and I enjoy the way things transform in front of you as you draw. They don’t really transform of course, but your perception does. Try it out.
